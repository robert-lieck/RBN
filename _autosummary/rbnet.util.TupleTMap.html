rbnet.util.TupleTMap

class rbnet.util.TupleTMap(arrs, *args, **kwargs)[source]

Bases: TMap

A tuple of TMap objects. All getters/setters of attributes and items return/take tuples of values. Internally, a TupleTMap object actually maintains a tuple of TMap objects and delegates all calls to these objects.
