rbnet.util.Prob

class rbnet.util.Prob(p, dim=None, raise_zero_norms=True, *args, **kwargs)[source]

Bases: Module, ConstrainedModuleMixin

A class for probability distributions that enforces positivity and normalisation constraints and projects the gradient in backward passes.

Variables:

  • p – probabilities

  • dim – dimensions along which normalisation is applied

Parameters:

  • p – initial probabilities

  • dim – dimensions along which normalisation is to be applied or None for all dimensions

  • args – passed on to super()__init__

  • kwargs – passed on to super()__init__
